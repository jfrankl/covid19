Open map data on US health system capacity to care for COVID-19 patients for all hospital facilities nationwide

We use open data on hospital bed capacity to provide a current view of the US health system's capacity to care for severely ill COVID-19 patients. See our project page for methodology notes, data sources, data processing notebooks, and code, all of which are open-sourced and freely available to use as is, adapt, and build on.
